Amenity Rules

Pool Rules
  • No lifeguard on duty – swim at your own risk.
  • Children under 12 must be supervised by an adult.
  • No food, drinks, glass containers, or animals inside the pool or surrounding areas (including the gazebo).
  • No events of any kind are permitted (including birthdays).
  • Only beach accessories (e.g., beach balls) are allowed; no tennis, football, soccer, or basketballs.
  • Shower before entering. Maximum capacity: 50 persons.
  • No running, diving, or horseplay allowed.
  • Pool hours: Sunrise to sunset. Night swimming is not permitted.
  • No alcoholic beverages or smoking/vaping.
  • No music speakers allowed; keep volume moderate.
BBQ Grill Rules
  • For residents only. First come, first served: 10 AM – 9 PM.
  • Minors under 18 must be supervised when using grills.
  • Grills must be attended at all times while in use.
  • Fully extinguish charcoal and clean area after use.
  • Report damage to management. Use at your own risk.
  • No loud music or external speakers; volume must remain moderate to respect community tranquility.
Clubhouse Rules
  • Only trained service animals are allowed, and must be under control; other animals are prohibited.
  • Children must be supervised at all times; this space is intended for responsible use, not as a play area.
  • Keep the Clubhouse clean, quiet, and organized; only non-alcoholic beverages with lids are allowed, and no food or group meetings.
  • Management may issue warnings, suspend access temporarily, or revoke privileges for repeated violations.
Work Lounge Rules
  • The Work Lounge is shared on a first-come, first-served basis; no monopolizing space.
  • Max capacity: 15 people. Hours: 6:00 AM – 11:00 PM.
  • Only trained service animals allowed; other animals are prohibited.
  • Notify management immediately of printer malfunctions.
  • Keep the lounge quiet, clean, and for individual use; no food or group meetings.
Gym Rules
  • Use equipment at your own risk; no liability for injuries.
  • Adults only; children over 12 must be supervised.
  • Wipe down equipment after use and rerack weights.
  • Dress code enforced: no topless use, no wet suits, no bare feet.
  • No food, smoking, drugs, or glass containers.
  • Report equipment malfunctions to management immediately.
Playground Rules
  • Use of playground equipment at your own risk.
  • Reserved for residents and guests only; adult supervision required.
  • No rough play or yelling; shoes required; no glass.
  • In emergencies, dial 911.
Dog Park Rules
  • Resident pets only; use at your own risk.
  • Dogs must be leashed when entering/exiting; cleaned up immediately.
  • Owners responsible for behavior; handlers 16+ years; children under 13 supervised.
  • Aggressive or sick dogs, puppies under 4 months, food/treats, glass containers prohibited.

Reglas de la Piscina
  • No hay socorrista – nade bajo su propio riesgo.
  • Niños <12 años deben estar supervisados.
  • No alimentos, bebidas, vidrio ni animales en la piscina o áreas cercanas.
  • No eventos de ningún tipo (incluidos cumpleaños).
  • Solo accesorios de playa permitidos; no pelotas de tenis, fútbol o baloncesto.
  • Dúchese antes de entrar. Capacidad máxima: 50 personas.
  • No correr, bucear ni juegos bruscos.
  • Horario: amanecer hasta atardecer. Prohibido nadar de noche.
  • No alcohol, fumar o vapear.
  • No altavoces; volumen moderado.
