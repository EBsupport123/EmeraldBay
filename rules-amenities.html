Amenity Rules

Pool Rules
  • No lifeguard on duty – swim at your own risk.
  • Children under 12 must be supervised by an adult.
  • No food, drinks, glass containers, or animals in pool area.
  • No events of any kind (e.g., birthdays).
  • Only beach accessories allowed; no balls (tennis, football, soccer, basketball).
  • Shower before entering. Max capacity: 50 persons.
  • No running, diving, or horseplay.
  • Pool hours: sunrise to sunset.
  • No alcohol or smoking/vaping.
  • No speakers; volume must remain moderate.
BBQ Grill Rules
  • Residents only. First come, first served (10 AM–9 PM).
  • Minors under 18 must be supervised.
  • Grills must be attended at all times.
  • Extinguish coals and clean area after use.
  • Report damage to management; use at your own risk.
  • No loud music or external speakers.
Clubhouse Rules
  • Only trained service animals allowed; others prohibited.
  • Children must be supervised; not a play area.
  • Keep space clean and quiet; only non-alcoholic beverages with lids; no food or meetings.
  • Management may warn, suspend, or revoke privileges for violations.
Work Lounge Rules
  • Shared first-come, first-served; no reserving.
  • Max capacity: 15. Hours: 6 AM–11 PM.
  • Only service animals allowed.
  • Notify management of printer issues.
  • Keep quiet and clean; no food or group meetings.
Gym Rules
  • Use equipment at your own risk.
  • Adults only; children 12+ supervised.
  • Wipe equipment and rerack weights.
  • Dress code: no topless, no wet suits, no bare feet.
  • No food, smoking, drugs, or glass.
  • Report malfunctions immediately.
Playground Rules
  • Use at your own risk; adult supervision required.
  • No rough play or yelling; shoes required; no glass.
  • Emergency? Call 911.
Dog Park Rules
  • Resident pets only; use at your own risk.
  • Leash when entering/exiting; clean up waste.
  • Owners responsible for behavior; handlers 16+; supervise children.
  • No aggressive/sick dogs, puppies under 4 months, food, or glass.

  • Niños <12 años deben ser supervisados.

  • No eventos (e.g., cumpleaños).

  • Dúchese antes de entrar. Capacidad máxima: 50.
