EMERALD BAY

Parking Regulations

Residents must notify the Leasing Office of any registration or plate changes and upload updated registration and insurance on the Parking Enforcement website, or park in a visitor space until complete.
Only vehicles displaying a valid parking decal may park on the property. Vehicles without a decal will be towed immediately.
General Parking Rules
  • Assigned spaces only.
  • Visitor spaces for guests, permitted hours only.
  • No on‑site mechanical work.
  • Motorcycles/scooters must display a permit.
  • No long‑term storage of inoperative vehicles.
  • Vehicles over 18 feet must rear‑end park in the designated rear area.
Immediate Towing Violations
  • Rear‑end parking
  • Parking on grass or sidewalk
  • Leaking fluids (fuel, oil, etc.)
  • No‑parking/fire‑zone lines
  • Blocking dumpster or mail area
  • Parking in the middle of the roadway
  • Parking in a handicap space without a handicap decal
  • Residents in visitor spots without authorization
  • Visitors in resident spots without authorization
  • Unregistered vehicle / no parking pass
  • Unauthorized overnight parking (11:00 PM – 7:00 AM)
  • Double‑parking / blocking another vehicle
  • Vehicles stored outside designated spots
  • Oversized vehicles over 18 feet parked outside rear area
  • Commercial vehicles over 6,000 lbs
Designated Parking Areas by Decal Color
  • Buildings 8550–8500–8400–8420–8430: Green zones/garage.
  • 8510–8540: Garage/rear area.
  • Any unit will only park in the assigned area.

Until 11:00 PM: Visitor parking without registration.

After 11:00 PM: Unregistered visitors towed immediately.

  • Up to 7 overnight registrations per plate.
  • Up to 12 visits per month.
