General Building Rules

Use & Conduct in Premises
  • Residential use only. No commercial activity or solicitation. Follow all applicable laws.
  • No shouting, loud music, strong odors, or harassment. Quiet hours: 11:00 PM–7:00 AM.
  • Tenants are responsible for their guests’ actions and any damages.
  • Children under 12 must be supervised. Guest stays: over 7 days register; over 14 days apply for tenancy; max 2 adults per bedroom + 1 child.
Safety & Storage
  • No flammable storage (e.g., gas-powered items, grills) in units, balconies, or common areas.
  • No access to utility areas or equipment without permission.
  • No items stored in hallways, entrances, stairwells, or public areas.
  • No drilling, screwing, or adhesives without permission; only approved hanging tools allowed.
  • Pets on balconies must be supervised at all times.
Access & Property Standards
  • No altering locks. $50 fee for lost or damaged mailbox keys.
  • No furniture stored in public areas. Only landlord-approved window coverings allowed.
  • No lawn parking, blocking, or repairs on premises. Clean leaks and remove vehicles.
  • Report leaks; conserve energy; do not remove screens.
  • Antennas or satellite dishes prohibited without written consent.
Plumbing, Storage & Disposal
  • Only toilet paper allowed in toilets. Tenants liable for misuse.
  • No flammable storage; landlord not responsible for items after lease ends.
  • $500 fine for improper bulky waste disposal (furniture/appliances).
  • Maintain units clean and sanitary.
  • No exterior signage or decor without permission.
Restricted Behaviors & Substances
  • Roof access strictly prohibited.
  • No waterbeds, aquariums, or unauthorized equipment.
  • Lofts not allowed.
  • No smoking indoors or within 15 feet of any building.
  • Do not tamper with, disable, or remove batteries from smoke detectors.
  • No possession, use, or sale of illegal drugs.
Maintenance, Clothing & Parking
  • Light bulbs provided at move-in; tenant responsible thereafter.
  • Landlord changes HVAC filters monthly; additional changes are tenant’s responsibility.
  • No display or use of firearms, knives, or similar weapons on property.
  • Proper attire required in public areas. No shirtless or barefoot individuals (except pool); no public alcohol use.
  • No commercial vehicles, boats, or RVs; guest parking requires visible passes; speed limit: 5 MPH.
Maintenance & Rentals
  • Submit maintenance requests via Tenant Portal. Tenant responsible for damages from violations.
  • Short-term rentals (e.g., Airbnb, Vrbo) strictly prohibited; violation leads to immediate termination and deposit forfeiture.
Trash & Recycling Rules
  • All trash must be securely bagged before disposal.
  • Do not place boxes in the chute; flatten and place in the designated first-floor waste room.
  • Follow recycling regulations; no toxic or combustible materials.
  • No car batteries, oils, or petroleum-based products.
  • No furniture or appliances near trash areas.
  • Keep all trash areas clean and free of litter.
Uso y Conducta en las Instalaciones
  • Solo uso residencial. No actividades comerciales o solicitudes. Cumpla con las leyes aplicables.
  • No gritar, música alta, olores fuertes ni acoso. Horas de silencio: 11:00 PM–7:00 AM.
  • Los inquilinos son responsables de las acciones de sus invitados y de cualquier daño.
  • Niños menores de 12 años con supervisión. Estancias: >7 días registrarse; >14 días solicitar arrendamiento; máximo 2 adultos por habitación +1 niño.
